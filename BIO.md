# TechnoPeace Bio

TechnoPeace is a sensory software project exploring calm, weather-aware interaction. It blends geolocation, atmospheric data, and reflective design into an instrument that feels less like a dashboard and more like a living place.

In its current phase, the project centers on **Sky Mode**: users enter through a brief visual ritual, then shape an always-on ambient drone where humidity, wind, cloud cover, sun angle, moon phase, and rainfall continuously color both tone and texture. The experience is intentionally gentle—built for slow attention, not productivity.

The broader vision is a modular ecology of tools across web, API, and shared packages, where environmental signals and personal reflection can become music, light, and narrative presence.
